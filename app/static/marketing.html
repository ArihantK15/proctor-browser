Student Experience

Designed to reduce
exam-day anxiety

One question at a time
No overwhelming 40-question scroll. Students see one question, answer it, and move on. Navigation grid gives a quick overview without distraction.
Camera preview that reassures
A small header-bar preview shows students their own camera feed at all times — "yes, the system can see me" — without dominating the question area.
Direct teacher chat
Students can message the invigilator mid-exam without leaving the window. No phone calls, no raised hands, no embarrassment in front of peers.
Offline-resilient save
Answers are saved locally every 15 seconds. If the internet drops mid-exam, no work is lost. The sync happens automatically on reconnect.
